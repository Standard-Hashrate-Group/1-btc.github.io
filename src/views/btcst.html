BTCST
Bitcoin Standard Hashrate Token
Bitcoin Standard Hashrate Token is the bridge between Bitcoin hashrate assets and the DeFi world. Each BTCST is collateralized with 0.1 TH/s of Bitcoin mining power. By staking BTCSTs, token holders are entitled to Bitcoin rewards and more.

BTCST Brings Bitcoin to DeFi
BTCST is an asset protocol that brings Bitcoin hashrate assets to DeFi. It serves as a strong foundation for other DeFi trading, lending, and borrowing protocols to build on.
Exchange Grade Liquidity
BTCST brings exchange‐grade liquidity to hashrate assets so that market participants can easily enter and exit mining exposure in any size and at any time.
Transparent Governance
BTCST is governed transparently as a DAO. Token holders, including our miner members and the broader token community, will collectively decide on material issues of the project, with all proposals, votes and outcomes preserved and disclosed on‐chain.
