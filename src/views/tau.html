Tau
A protocol to synthesize PoW assets
τ is a protocol to synthesize proof‐of‐work cryptocurrencies. By synthesizing the mining process, the τ protocol creates synthetic proof‐of‐work cryptocurrencies with provable value support and strong mechanisms to restore price pegs.
